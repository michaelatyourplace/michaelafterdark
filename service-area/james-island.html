James Island Neighborhoods — Full Coverage

Riverland Terrace

Northern section · Riverland Drive · Ashley River access

Established mid-century homes under mature live oaks, less than 2 miles from the Connector. One of the island's most desirable and characterful neighborhoods.

Seaside Farms & The Villages

Central island · Folly Road (SC-171)

Townhomes and single-family neighborhoods representing the island's suburban growth from 1980–2010. Pools, parks, and easy Folly Road access in every direction.

Lighthouse Point & Marshview

Southern waterfront · Folly Road approach

Premium waterfront homes with boat docks and Charleston Harbor views — the island's upscale residential corridor heading toward Folly Beach.

Camp Road & Grimball Road

Western & southern island · Maybank Hwy connection

Larger lots and open space connecting to Johns Island, Kiawah, and Seabrook. Camp Road's commercial hub at Folly Road serves the whole southwest island.

Access Routes — Every Direction Covered

James Island Connector (SC-30)

The 3.5-mile limited-access highway from Folly Road directly to downtown Charleston's Lockwood Drive. 4-6 minute crossing on a typical evening — the fastest route to King Street, Broad Street, and the French Quarter.

Primary route to downtown Charleston bookings

Folly Road (SC-171)

The direct 10+ mile scenic highway from the Connector through the island's commercial heart all the way to Folly Beach — passing restaurants, shops, and the island's main business corridor along the way.

Direct access to Folly Beach vacation rentals

Maybank Highway (SC-700)

Southern connection to Johns Island, Kiawah Island, and Seabrook Island. Also serves as an alternate routing option to avoid peak Connector traffic during special Charleston events.

Access to Kiawah resort properties & Johns Island

Wappoo Road / SC-7

Northern connection to West Ashley's Avondale neighborhood and an alternate downtown route along scenic Wappoo Creek. Passes South Windermere for convenient commercial access.

Alternate downtown route & West Ashley access
