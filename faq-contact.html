Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking Michael for your Charleston area bachelorette party or private celebration.

What type of entertainment does Michael provide?
Michael is a professional male entertainer specializing in bachelorette parties and private celebrations throughout Charleston County. The service provides upscale, respectful entertainment for groups celebrating in vacation rentals, hotel suites, and private homes across Charleston, Mount Pleasant, Isle of Palms, Folly Beach, and surrounding Lowcountry areas.
Where are you based and what areas do you serve?
Michael at Your Place operates from Mount Pleasant, South Carolina, strategically positioned to serve the entire Charleston County celebration market. Service areas include:
  • Charleston: Downtown peninsula, Upper King Street, French Quarter, West Ashley, James Island
  • Isle of Palms: Wild Dunes, Front Beach, oceanfront rentals
  • Folly Beach: All vacation rental areas and beach houses
  • Sullivan's Island: Beach properties and residential rentals
  • Mount Pleasant: Local residences and event spaces
Travel time from Mount Pleasant base: 10-15 minutes to downtown Charleston via Ravenel Bridge, 15-20 minutes to Isle of Palms via IOP Connector, 30 minutes to Folly Beach.
What neighborhoods in Charleston do you serve?
Service covers all Charleston neighborhoods including Upper King Street hotel district, French Quarter historic properties, South of Broad elegant homes, Cannonborough-Elliotborough rentals, Harleston Village near College of Charleston, Radcliffeborough residential areas, West Ashley's Avondale and Byrnes Down neighborhoods, and James Island vacation rentals near Folly Road and Riverland Drive.

During summer months (July-August) and off-season (November-March), 2-3 weeks advance notice typically works. Last-minute bookings are sometimes available depending on schedule — contact directly to check availability.

Quick Charleston Area Tips

Parking in downtown Charleston: If your rental is in Upper King, French Quarter, or South of Broad, expect limited street parking. Consider Uber/Lyft for your group's evening plans.

Best bachelorette neighborhoods: Upper King Street for walkable nightlife access, James Island for pools and space, Isle of Palms for beach vibes and upscale feel, Folly Beach for laid-back coastal party atmosphere.

Timing around Charleston traffic: Ravenel Bridge evening traffic clears by 7 PM on weekdays. Weekend evenings have minimal delays. Isle of Palms Connector traffic peaks 4-6 PM on summer Fridays but is clear by evening entertainment hours.
