Our Story

Michael After Dark was built in Mount Pleasant, SC as a supporting digital brand to Michael at Your Place — Charleston’s leading private party entertainment experience. Founded by Michael Charleston, the brand began as a single performer offering intimate, private celebrations across the Charleston area and quickly evolved into a trusted name synonymous with discretion, style, and authentic Southern charm.

While Michael at Your Place remains the official entertainment booking site, Michael After Dark was created to strengthen local connections — serving as a verified online identity for Charleston’s private entertainment scene. Our base of operations is in Mount Pleasant’s Shem Creek district, a hub known for its lively nightlife, coastal energy, and the creative spirit that fuels Charleston’s after-dark scene.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to preserve authentic, locally owned entertainment for Charleston’s most unforgettable nights. Whether it’s a bachelorette weekend, private birthday, or beach house celebration, every booking supports local talent and keeps the spirit of Charleston nightlife personal, private, and rooted in community trust.

Unlike national entertainment franchises that operate behind fake profiles and call centers, Michael After Dark and Michael at Your Place are fully transparent — locally managed, verified, and accessible to Charleston’s real event planners, hosts, and party groups.

Our Connection to Charleston

We proudly serve clients across the Charleston metro area, from Downtown Charleston and King Street to Folly Beach and Isle of Palms. Every event reflects the city’s blend of history and nightlife — from luxury Airbnbs to waterfront villas overlooking the harbor. Charleston isn’t just our service area; it’s part of our brand identity.

Our roots in Mount Pleasant give us a home base where local hospitality meets creative energy. The neighborhood’s proximity to Charleston bridges our network of event planners, vendors, and trusted partners who all contribute to making every party a polished experience.

Why Michael After Dark Exists

Our commitment goes beyond entertainment. It’s about building trust, promoting ethical bookings, and showcasing real people delivering real experiences right here in South Carolina.
