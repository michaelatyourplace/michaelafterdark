About Us

Locally Operated in Mount Pleasant — Serving Charleston’s Most Exclusive Private Events

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to preserve authentic, locally owned entertainment for Charleston’s most unforgettable nights. Whether it’s a girls weekend, private birthday, or beach house celebration, every booking supports local talent and keeps the spirit of Charleston nightlife personal, private, and rooted in community trust.

Unlike national entertainment franchises that operate behind fake profiles and call centers, After Dark and Michael After Dark are fully transparent — locally managed, verified, and accessible to Charleston’s real event planners, hosts, and party groups.

Our Connection to Charleston

We proudly serve clients across the Charleston metro area, from Downtown Charleston and King Street to Folly Beach and Isle of Palms. Every event reflects the city’s blend of history and nightlife — from luxury Airbnbs to waterfront villas overlooking the harbor. Charleston isn’t just our service area; it’s part of our brand identity.

Our roots in Mount Pleasant give us a home base where local hospitality meets creative energy. The neighborhood’s proximity to Charleston bridges our network of event planners, vendors, and trusted partners who all contribute to making every party a polished experience.

Why We Exists

Our commitment goes beyond entertainment. It’s about building trust, promoting ethical bookings, and showcasing real people delivering real experiences right here in South Carolina.
